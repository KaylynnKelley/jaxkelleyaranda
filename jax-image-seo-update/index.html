03 — The Player

Beats his man. Then makes the difference.

Jax Kelley-Aranda is a highly competitive, versatile attacker playing for Crossfire Premier — a national ECNL Club of the Year with multiple ECNL Championships to its name. Cultivated in that championship environment, he thrives on high-stakes competition.

He primarily anchors the pitch as a left winger, but his tactical flexibility lets him slide to the right or step into midfield to disrupt defenses and dictate tempo. Right-footed with a strong left, he's comfortable attacking down either flank.

Off the field, he carries a 3.59 weighted GPA and plans to pursue a degree in Business. He's looking for a collegiate home that challenges him as an elite athlete and a scholar — and he's ready to impact a high-level roster from day one.
